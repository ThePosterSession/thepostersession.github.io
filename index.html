People and Community

The Poster Session is intended to bringing people together and connecting, very much like in a poster session of an academic conference. Unlike previous events, there are no speakers and not one single communication flow. The event is free of charge, with food and drinks provided. A “poster” is simply a 3’x 4’ board on which you have information about what your company does.


Academia and Industry

Learn about new technologies and discoveries made in companies or research centers, or come show your latest achievements.



Deadlines

Poster, demo submissions and RSVPs close Novemeber 9th. To register as a regular attendee, click the RSVP link above. To register as a Presenter, please create an account at https://www.easychair.org. Once created, you can submit your poster or demo by navigating to the PS2015 page. Please keep in mind that posters should focus on communicating your work and how it relates to the industry. Demos are encouraged but not required. If you plan to give a demo please let us know in your submission so we can plan for it. Due to space limitations, we may have to limit acceptance. Although space for your poster and WiFi will be provided, you should bring your own laptop or tablet for your presentation or demonstration.
